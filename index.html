About Us

TRENCE IT is a B2B IT solutions company supplying software systems to small and mid-sized enterprises as well as startup ventures, in order to alleviate customers of the time and resource consuming task of building an in-house IT team. We create high quality solutions faster and cheaper than both our competitors and our customers in-house teams. Our Swedish, Indonesian and Bosnian based teams of expert developers will guarantee that the development of our systems will result in very high customer satisfaction.

In order to achieve the highest level of client satisfaction, our executive branch is close to our client base in northern Europe in order to ensure complete and efficient communication as well as transparent cooperation. Our clients have complete access to our teams and services, enabling them to have full control and insight while the development of their systems is taken care of by our crew of expert developers based in Indonesia and Bosnia.
