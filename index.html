About Us

TRENCE is an IT solutions company based in Stockholm, Sweden. We specialise in software systems for small to mid-sized enterprises and startup ventures, so you as an entrepreneur don’t have to take the time and resources required to find the right competences. We have decades of experience in creating high quality solutions and we work faster and cheaper than both our competitors and your ordinary in-house IT team. Our Swedish, Indonesian and Bosnian based teams of expert developers will guarantee that your IT is working efficiently, so you can focus on growth and prosperity.

What do we do?
We create IT solutions tailor made for our clients, utilising our competent teams with decades of experience within multiple fields of technologies. How can we help you?
Maybe you have an idea for a system that you want to develop, or maybe you have an organisation that needs a new IT team or to expand your It operations. Regardless, putting together an IT team is a serious challenge for everyone. We take great pleasure in developing your ideas and making your ambitions come true. By teaming up with us you will free up time, energy and money and you will be guaranteed an efficient process with a satisfactory result.

Why TRENCE

Because we don’t just serve our clients, we partner with them. Our business strategy is built on teaming up with our clients long term, with clients returning to TRENCE when they see the need and that there will not be a doubt which IT solutions company they will want to work with for their future projects. This requires a superior customer satisfaction, something we will work tirelessly to achieve. We are a Swedish company based in Stockholm so the communication with our clients will flow efficiently and without culture barriers.

The fact that we charge less than our competitors is also an advantage.
